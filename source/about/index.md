## RimWorld-zh 边缘汉化组

边缘汉化组是由风之起灵创建的翻译团队。  
我们致力于边缘世界模组的翻译，包括**「简体中文」**与**「繁体中文」**。

The RimWorld-zh is a translation team constructed by duduluu.  
We focus on translating RimWorld Mods, including both of Simplified Chinese and Tranditianal Chinese.
